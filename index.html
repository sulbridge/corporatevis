Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does CorporateVis do?
CorporateVis is a full‑service enterprise AI visibility and reputation firm. We do everything — you just get the results.
How is this different from hiring an agency?
Agencies charge 2–5× more, take weeks to respond, and focus on single locations rather than your entire portfolio. We offer fixed, transparent pricing, 48‑hour response, and dedicated support for your whole network. We also provide white‑label reports you can share directly with your leadership team.
How quickly will we see results?
Most enterprise clients see measurable improvements in their AI Visibility Score within 2–4 weeks. Within 30–60 days, you'll see increased inquiries, bookings, and revenue as ChatGPT, Gemini, and other AI platforms start recommending your brand more prominently across all locations.
What does 'AI Visibility Score' mean?
Our proprietary AI Visibility Score (0–100) measures how prominently your brand appears in AI recommendations across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Copilot, and Grok. It's calculated from 9 reputation signals that AI systems use to evaluate trustworthiness. A score above 80 means AI consistently recommends your brand.
How do you measure ROI?
We track three core metrics: (1) AI Visibility Score growth (2) share of voice vs. competitors (3) revenue from AI‑driven leads. We provide a monthly ROI report showing exactly how much new business came from AI recommendations — so you know exactly what you're getting.

You own all your data and reports. We provide a 30‑day transition period with full documentation and handover support. There are no lock‑in contracts — you stay because you see value, not because you're trapped.
Do you work with our existing agencies?
Absolutely. We partner with your current SEO, PR, or marketing agencies. We focus exclusively on AI visibility and reputation signals — we complement, not compete. We share data and insights so your entire ecosystem works together.
How do you ensure data security?
We adhere to strict security protocols including SOC 2 compliance, data encryption at rest and in transit, and signed confidentiality agreements. Your data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent.
