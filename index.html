Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about AI visibility for enterprises.

What is AI visibility and why does it matter for enterprises?
AI visibility is how often and how positively your brand is mentioned, cited, and recommended in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, etc.). For enterprises, it directly impacts brand perception, trust, and customer acquisition — buyers increasingly ask AI for recommendations before visiting your website.
How is ReputationGuardPro Enterprise different from traditional SEO tools?
Traditional SEO tools focus on keyword rankings and backlinks. ReputationGuardPro Enterprise is built for the AI era: we track brand mentions, sentiment, citation share, and passage-level optimization across 8+ AI platforms. We also close the loop — from audit to implementation to verification.
How do you guarantee our data is secure?
All agents operate read‑only by default. Publishing, theme edits, and redirects require your explicit approval. Every action is logged in a tamper‑evident audit trail. We're also SOC2 compliant (in progress) and align with GDPR/CCPA.
What kind of results can we expect and how fast?
Most clients see measurable improvements within 4–8 weeks: increased mention frequency, improved sentiment, and higher citation share. Our 30‑day Score Delta report quantifies the lift. Enterprise clients often see 100‑300% growth in AI‑driven traffic within 3 months.
