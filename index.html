FAQ

Questions worth asking before you buy.

Can the agent break my site?
Default access is read‑and‑draft only — the agent audits and stages changes, but never publishes on its own. Publishes, theme edits, redirects, and robots.txt changes all require your explicit click‑to‑approve. Every change is logged to a tamper‑evident audit trail and is one‑click reversible.
Do you train on my content?
Not by default. For Done‑For‑You engagements, training on de‑identified outputs is per‑engagement, unchecked by default, and used only if you opt in. When you do, payloads are de‑identified before storage and the consent is revocable. We never sell your data.
What CMSes do you support today?
WordPress (native draft), Shopify, Webflow, Drupal, Magento, Joomla, and GitHub/GitLab PR mode for headless stacks. Squarespace and Wix run in assist mode — copy‑paste blocks with guidance. Any other CMS falls back to assist mode.
How do you measure "share of model"?
We run weekly prompt probes across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews, and Google SERP — and deliver the report: citation frequency, rank, and exact answer snippets. You get the findings, not a dashboard to dig through. Your page is also scored for AI‑readiness across schema, content, trust, and GEO dimensions.
